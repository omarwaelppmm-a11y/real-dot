# Project Title: The Ring

## Event: BEEST

## Author: Omar Wael

## How to play the game:

The game is played by controlling your player using the keyboard to move, aim, and survive against waves of tracking enemies. Use your mouse to point and shoot your projectile weapons, and collect the rotating honey jars that spawn around the map to heal your damage before they vanish. 

Wear your headphones for smooth playing and sound on maximum.
Try to focus on every detail <3

## Why I made it: 

Made it to create a tense, high-energy arcade survival experience. Balancing the enemy tracking logic with custom boundary mechanics kept me on my toes, and tweaking the survival loop while testing was incredibly fun.

## Features <3

* Action-packed 2D arcade survival and high-energy gameplay loop
* Smooth procedural shader animations, visual scaling, and custom UI layouts
* Modular background music system that persists across menus, gameplay, and shop screens
* Dynamic weapon mechanics and temporary spawning pickup systems

Made for BEEST.
